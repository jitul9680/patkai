PATKAI MEN'S HOSTEL

"...Swargadeo Sukafa,the first Ahom king of Assam came to this land crossing the Patkai mountain range,and laid the foundation of BOR AXOM... "

"...In the same way we the boarders of Patkai are here to enlighten ourselves with specialized knowledge and lay the foundation for a bright future... "

The Patkai Men's Hostel was inaugurated in the auspicious day of 23rd July 2011 by Shri Srikant Kumar Jena,Hon'ble Union minister of state for Statistics and programme implementation.The hostel with a magnificient building sprawling over a large area is a masterpiece in itself.Patkai Men's Hostel boasts of excellent infrastructure with a fully equipped Common Room,with Sports Kits readily available to the boarders. Patkai Men's Hostel is presently one of the largest men's hostel of Tezpur University having the strength to accommodate 440+ boarders.
